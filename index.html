About Me

I am a Master’s student in Mathematics at the University of Münster. My research interests center on homotopy theory, group homology, and K-theory - fields whose 'language' I am currently immersing myself in. That said, I am drawn to the full breadth of mathematics, and I find the applications of algebra and topology to other disciplines.

Previously, I graduated with distinction from the Faculty of Mathematics and Computer Science at Saint Petersburg State University.

Beyond the world of math, I have a wide range of passions, including theater, cinema, football, chess, and linguistics, and ...
